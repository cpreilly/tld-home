VENTILATED AIR JERSEY

It's hot out there. You need to stay cool to perform at your best. The SE Air Gear is designed to allow optimum air flow and pull heat away from your body, cooling you to the core when it matters most.

The crown jewel of the TLD gear line, the SE Air gear was developed to provide riders with an extraordinary balance between lightweight feel and bulletproof strength. State of the art, technologically advanced materials provide maximum durability, ventilation and unprecidented fit. SE Air gear is the embodiment of the very best research, development and ingenuity that TLD has to offer.

SE Air Jersey Features:
  • Lightweight ventilated polyester mesh front and back
  • Ventilated polyester mesh sleeves
