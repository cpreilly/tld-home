CYCLING TRANSIT JACKET

The Transit soft shell jacket provides weather protection and beathability. Perfect for cold weather aerobic activity; unzip the vents and let the air in when conditions change. This technical wind-proof and highly water resistant jacket is the go-to jacket for uncertain weather conditions. Featuring taped interior seams, plenty of accessible storage and a drop-tail for extra coverage.

Transit Jacket Features:
  • 95% polyester / 5% spandex 2-way stretch DWR coated outer shell fabric bonded to brushed tricot
  • Taped intrior seams
  • 2-way waterproof YKK front zipper allows for venting
  • Underarm vents, easy access upper arm pocket and rear storage pocket with YKK waterproof zippers
  • Rear venting panel allows for excess body heat to escape
  • Water resistant media pocket
  • Front hand pockets with YKK waterproof zippers
  • Interior Lycra cuffs lock out wind and water
  • Interior brushed micro fiber zipper garage
  • Drop tail rear
