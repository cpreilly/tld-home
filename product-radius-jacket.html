LIGHTWEIGHT OFFROAD JACKET

To ensure added safety to the rider we use Retro-Reflective material which reflects light back to the source and illuminates the rider. Retro-reflective material provides additional visual cues to others and are far more effective than just bright clothing alone. Jacket comes with multiple waterproof zippered chest, and rear exhaust vents. Jacket also provides waterproof zippered storage pockets. Padded elbow pads.

Adventure Radius Jacket Features:
  • 420 denier water resistant oxford upper torso and top of sleeves
  • 300 denier water resistant oxford polyester torso and underarms
  • Waterproof YKK® zippered breast storage pocket
  • YKK® zippered vent and hand warmer pockets
  • Internalized elastic waist gather
  • Removable sleeves
  • Velcro® wrist closures
  • Rear integrated storage pocket for sleeves and supplies
  • Integrated lower zippered expansion pleats
  • Reflective logo on front and rear for night sections
  • 600 denier polyester padded elbow panels
