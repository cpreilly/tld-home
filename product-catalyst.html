LIGHTWEIGHT PROTECTIVE KNEE BRACES

These lightweight, easy to use knee braces will protect your knees when you run out of talent, and give you confidence to ride at a higher level. Custom autoconform aluminum frame will feel snug and molded to the contours of your legs.

Catalyst Knee Brace Features:
  • Set of left and right knee braces
  • 7075 Series frame is light, slim and durable
  • Tibial Docking System™ provides secure tibial control
  • Moldable aluminum frame enables customization
  • Autoconform aluminum-core wings adapt to anatomy
  • Integrated PCL strap delivers increased stability
  • Extension stops: 0°, 5°, 10°, 15°, 20°, and 25°
  • Flexion stops: 45°, 60°, 75°, and 90°
  • Full coverage patella guard is removable for washing
  • Moisture wicking frame liners, condyle pads and strap pads keep you cool and comfortable
  • Open medial upper and lower shells provide maximum clearance and comfort on the medial aspect of both legs, making it the perfect solution for athletes engaging in activities such as mountain biking and motocross
